About

Marlon Vera is an Ecuadorian mixed martial artist who competes in the bantamweight division of the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC). He was born on December 2, 1992, in Chone, Ecuador, and began training in martial arts at the age of 12.

Vera made his professional MMA debut in 2012 and quickly established himself as a talented fighter, earning a record of 8-2 before being signed by the UFC in 2014. Since then, he has faced a number of tough opponents and has become known for his aggressive fighting style and strong ground game.

One of Vera's most memorable moments in the UFC came in August 2020, when he defeated rising star Sean O'Malley in a thrilling upset victory. Vera has also earned performance bonuses for his impressive wins over Brad Pickett, Brian Kelleher, and Andre Ewell.

Outside of the octagon, Vera is known for his strong work ethic and dedication to his family. He often speaks about the importance of his wife and daughter in his life and credits them with motivating him to train hard and pursue his dreams.

Overall, Marlon Vera is a talented and exciting fighter who has quickly become a fan favorite in the UFC. With his impressive skills and humble personality, he is sure to continue making waves in the sport for years to come.
